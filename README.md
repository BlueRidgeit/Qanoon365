# Qanoon365

AI-powered legal intelligence platform for UAE law firms. BlueRidge IT branded.

**Web:** https://qanoon365-web-dev.victoriousdesert-c704d4be.eastus2.azurecontainerapps.io
**API:** https://qanoon365-api-dev.victoriousdesert-c704d4be.eastus2.azurecontainerapps.io

## What's in the box

- Full legal CRM (clients, matters, engagements, conflicts, KYC, documents)
- **Daleel** AI chatbot — RAG-powered legal research over firm documents
- Court Intelligence Engine — party profiling, case comparison, opposing counsel analysis
- AI Document Drafting — engagement letters, memos, NDAs, demand letters
- AI Conflict Detection — auto-check with fuzzy Arabic name matching
- AI Intake Assistant — raw text to structured lead data
- Document ingestion pipeline — SharePoint to Azure AI Search (OCR, chunking, embeddings)
- Multi-tenant (schema-per-tenant PostgreSQL)
- English + Arabic with full RTL support

## Tech

NestJS 11 | Next.js 16 | Prisma 7 | PostgreSQL 16 | Azure OpenAI GPT-4o | Azure AI Search | Python 3.12
